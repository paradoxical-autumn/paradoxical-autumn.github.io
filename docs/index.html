WHO AM I?

a programmer and musician who draws and does 3d stuff sometimes

Hi! I'm Autumn. I'm a programmer from the UK who also spends time making artsy stuff! I don't just program as I'm good at it, I program as I want to do it. I share this passion along with everything else in life, whether it be hobbies, jobs or whatever else.

Outside of coding, I also enjoy digital art, composing music and 3D texturing. You can view these on my alternate portfolio.
